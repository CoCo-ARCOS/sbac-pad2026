# Call for Tutorials Proposals

The 38th IEEE/SBC International Symposium on Computer Architecture and High Performance Computing (SBAC-PAD) provide an opportunity for experts from academia, industry, and research centers to present cutting-edge topics in parallel, distributed, and high-performance computing. Tutorials should offer participants an in-depth understanding of emerging technologies, tools, and methodologies that shape the field.

## Important dates

* Deadline for Tutorial Proposals: April 15th, 2026 (AoE)
* Notification of acceptance: April 30th, 2026

## Submission Guidelines

Tutorials proposals must be submitted in PDF format and must include:

* Tutorial name and duration (half-day or full-day).
* Brief description of the tutorial (0.5 – 1 page).
* List of topics covered and their relevance to the SBAC-PAD community.
* Organizers’ details: names, affiliations, and short bios (5-10 lines).
* Tentative program (if available).
* Expected structure and activities.
