# Visa Information

Conference participants may be required to obtain a visa to enter Spain. Please consult this document for more information: [ANNEX I - LIST OF THIRD COUNTRIES WHOSE NATIONALS ARE REQUIRED TO BE IN POSSESSION OF A VISA - Page 14](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/PDF/?uri=CELEX:02018R1806-20250203).

In case of requiring an invitation letter to attend the conference, please let us know.
