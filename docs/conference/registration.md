## Registration fees

| Registration Category | Advance registration (until September 15th, 2026) | Late / On site |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **IEEE Member** | 600 € | 700 € |
| **Non IEEE Member** | 720 € | 840 € |
| **Student, IEEE Member** | 420 € | 490 € |
| **Student, non IEEE Member** | 505 € | 590 € |
| **IEEE Life Member** | 330 € | 385 € |

Members of the Brazilian Computer Society may pay the same fee as the corresponding IEEE membership.

Each accepted paper requires a separate full conference registration (i.e. at either the IEEE Life Member, IEEE/SBC Member, or Non-member rate).

Author registrations should be identified by answering yes to the question “Is your registration linked to an abstract or a workshop?” and providing the EasyChair paper number assigned to your paper as the answer to the question “What is your abstract number?” on the online registration form.

Authors will be charged a total of 100 € for extra pages over the limit of 10 pages for main conference papers.

## Certificate of attendance
Individual certificates of attendance will be sent during the conference, directly to the e-mail address used for registration. If you need any additional certificate (certificate of presentation for example), please contact Javier García - <fjblas@inf.uc3m.es>.
